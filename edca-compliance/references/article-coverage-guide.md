## Proportional Application (Art. 39)

Per Art. 39º, obligations in Art. 6º, 17, 18, 19, 20, 27, 28, 29, 31, 32, and 40 apply **proportionally** based on:
- Product/service characteristics and functionality
- Degree of content interference/moderation capability
- Number of users
- Provider size

### Exemptions (Art. 39º, §1º)
Providers of **services with editorial control** and **licensed copyrighted content** are exempt from certain obligations if:
- I - Observe classification guidelines
- II - Provide age classification transparency
- III - Offer parental mediation mechanisms (content, data, interaction, commerce restrictions)

## Quick Applicability Decision Tree

1. **Is it a product/service of information technology?** (Art. 2º, I)
   → If No: EDCA does not apply
   → If Yes: Continue

2. **Is it directed at children/adolescents OR likely accessed by them?** (Art. 1º)
   → If No to both: EDCA does not apply
   → If Yes to either: EDCA applies

3. **Check "Likely Access" criteria** (Art. 1º, parágrafo único):
   - I - Sufficient probability of use + attractiveness
   - II - Considerable ease of access + use
   - III - Significant risk to privacy/security/development
   → Any one criterion met = "likely access" = EDCA applies

4. **Apply proportional obligations** (Art. 39º) based on provider size, user count, content interference degree
